The Simpsons Fan Page

The Simpsons is an American animated sitcom created by Matt Groening. The series is a satirical depiction of working-class life, epitomized by the Simpson family, which consists of Homer, Marge, Bart, Lisa, and Maggie. The show is set in the fictional town of Springfield and parodies American culture and society, television, and the human condition. The Simpsons Movie, a feature-length film, was released in theaters worldwide on July 27, 2007. The Simpsons are mostly Known for their prediction which sometimes do indeed come true.
